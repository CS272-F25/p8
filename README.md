## Purpose & Goals

* Make organizing Secret Santa exchanges simple and stress-free
* Encourage creativity and participation through interactive features
* Foster connection and community during the holiday season
* Create a fun, festive experience tailored to busy college life

---

## Key Features

* **User Registration**: Participants can sign up to join a Secret Santa exchange
* **Matching System**: Users are paired for gift exchanges through an interactive process
* **Present Hunt (Scavenger Hunt)**: A playful element that adds anticipation and engagement before the final reveal
* **Gift Ideas Page**: Provides inspiration to help users find thoughtful gifts
* **About Us Page**: Explains the mission, values, and team behind the project
* **Contact Page**: Allows users to reach out with questions or feedback
